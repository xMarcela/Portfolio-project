The Road to Coding


I'm Aleksandra Marcela Ravn, so call me Marcela.


Originally from Chile and raised in Norway, I have a background in design through Fashion & Makeup, Clothing Design, Hairdressing educations.

After years in the service industry as a bartender and manager, seamstress and withing retail, with Covid 19 and being at home it was time to explore new passions.

I wanted to learn something new and creative. Deciding to build upon my education and problem solving interests cultivated through video games and climbing - mixed together with my natural curiousity I decided to start study UI- UX design and from there, into Coding and Web Development.
