The Road to Coding


I'm Aleksandra Marcela Ravn, so call me Marcela.


Originally from Chile and raised in Norway, I have a background in design through Fashion & Makeup, Clothing Design, Hairdressing educations.

After years in the service industry as a bartender and manager, seamstress and within retail, with Covid 19 and being at home it was time to explore new passions.

I wanted to learn something new and creative. Deciding to build upon my education and problem solving interests cultivated through video games and climbing - mixed together with my natural curiousity I decided to start study a year of UI- UX design and from there, into Coding and Web Development.


Coding & Web Development


The past 4 months I've worked on my skills with SheCodes workshops. Learning the process of coding through a good mix of theory and practic approach. I am currently working on completing their Responsive Web Development course, before going onto the React course.
